Article Generation Help
Article Generation Process
  1. Articles are generated on a weekly basis
  2. Each article summarizes Ethereum development activity for one week
  3. Content is gathered from GitHub repositories, forums, and other sources
  4. Articles are automatically processed to ensure readability
Generation Options
  • Generate Single Article: Creates an article for the current week if none exists, or the most recent past week without an article
  • Generate Previous Articles: Generates multiple articles for past weeks, select the number of weeks from the dropdown
  • New Article: Manually create and edit an article
Status Indicators
  • Generating - Article is currently being created
  • Published - Article is complete and visible
  • Draft - Article is saved but not published
Article Generation Schedule

New articles are automatically generated every Monday at 9:00 UTC. Articles will only be generated if there isn't already an article for that week. The scheduler can be controlled using the manage_scheduler.py script: 

# Enable automatic article generation
python scripts/manage_scheduler.py enable

# Disable automatic article generation
python scripts/manage_scheduler.py disable
